Welcome To Simon

An digital verison of the electronic game invented by Ralph H. Baer and Howard J. Morrison.

How To Play

Simon is a game to test a player's memory. At the start of each level, the computer will flash a sequence of buttons that the player must then press in the same order. If the player enters the sequence correctly, he or she will move on to the next level and the length of the sequence will increase by one. If at any time the player presses a button in the incorrect order, then game is over.
